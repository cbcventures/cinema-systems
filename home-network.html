POPULAR QUESTIONS

100mbps router or 1gbps router?
It depends on the workload of your router and your network consumption, but these days we recommend the fastest router spec available, which is the 1gbps routers.  Especially if you plan on having many wireless devices connect to it while you stream, download, and upload, it’s best to go with the higher speed router to avoid bottlenecks.
What can I do to avoid spotty WiFi signals or dead spots in my house?
WiFi signals sometimes don’t work so great depending on the size of the house, or materials used (for example marbles, rocks can interfere with the signal).  There  are things we can do to bring healthy WiFi strength to the dead zones, with use of repeaters, and power of ethernet.
Do you perform security audit on our home network?
With so much security breaches going on in the world, let our CCNA and MSP experts secure your network against brute force attacks, MAC or IP spoofing, and incorporate series of best practices to your home network.
