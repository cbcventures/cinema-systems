SMART HOME AUTOMATION

9/13/2022

0 Comments

 
SMART HOME AUTOMATION: HOW DOES IT ENSURES SAFE AND PEACEFUL LIFE FOR HOMEOWNERS?The automated smart home automation market is said to reach USD 313.95 billion by the year 2027. It shows the market will grow at a CAGR of 25.3% from 2022 to 2027. This is a remarkable move, as these types of homes can keep your property safe when you are away. Increased use of Smartphones and internet penetration along with the introduction of IoT has made things easier. There are smart devices that you can keep in your house to use their special features when you are out on a vacation.
Let’s check out some cool smart home devices to give you a peaceful, happy, and safe life.
Smart BulbsSmart light bulbs had removed the major inconvenience for many families if they have forgot to turn off the lights for a specific room. They last more than the regular bulbs somewhere around 10 times more. The best part of smart bulbs is you can turn them on or off, dim their lights, and sometimes change their color with the help of a smartphone. So you can even trick the people by turning the light off and on; when you are away on vacation if you notice any moment around the house. It will give an impression that someone is at home.
Smart Security SystemIt operates with the help of Wi-Fi. So smart home security solutions help to perform relatable tasks; such as motion trackers, see-through cameras, speakers, and motion trackers. Most video doorbells work as a smart home automation security system. So you can download the app on your phone, and mount the system at a place from where you can get a view of your exteriors. The security cameras attached to these security systems provide high-quality images day and night.
Smoke AlarmsAnother important smart device is the smoke alarm that helps to safeguard your loved ones from toxic gases, fatal fires, and carbon monoxide in the house. The smoke alarms detect smoke in your home, alert you through the notification on your smartphone, and save your life. A few smoke alarms are battery-powered, and others are wired models. It helps you to send SOS messages to the emergency services quickly.
Smart LocksWith these kinds of locks, you do not have to worry anymore. You can be out for your vacation, and control lock system for the whole house remotely. You can unlock or lock the home with your Smartphone only. These locks let you know which lock in your home is open and in which room. Even digital keys can be provided to the person who is caring for your home in your absence.
ConclusionHackers are everywhere, so your smart home can be breached at any moment. The best is to keep strong passcodes, and make sure the router controlling the whole house is encrypted. Thus, two-factor authentication and routine check of your internet setting also prove helpful.

4 EFFECTIVE TIPS

8/11/2022

0 Comments

 
4 EFFECTIVE TIPS HELPING YOU IN BUILDING A PERFECT HOME THEATER SYSTEMWe all love watching movies, especially in ambiances created only to complement those entertaining hours. Therefore, wise people have already chosen home theater systems over visiting movie theaters repeatedly. However, the wholesome experience is only obtainable when you arrange your home theater system without making any mistakes. Hence, if you love acquiring movie-theater-like experiences in your dwelling and don’t want unnecessary flaws in your home theater arrangement, the following tips are just for you. So here are 4 effective tips.
Select the Location ProperlySelecting an ideal place for your custom home theater is significant, as you don’t want your movie watching and home business intervening with each other. Hence, make sure you have at least a 13 feet wide and 20 feet long relatively secluded area in your home where you can set your home theater up.
 Choosing the Right GadgetsProjectors are the best options if you want your frame size to be more than 50 inches. Various Full HD and Ultra HD 4K options are available these days. Besides, many are also purchasing models with 3D adaptability. So, you might need to purchase some 3D glasses in that case. Or, you can go with a big-screen TV with Ultra HD 4K, OLED, or Edge LED features.
Oh, and don’t forget to purchase subscriptions to the popular OTT platforms like NetFlix, Amazon Instant, and more. It’s the best time to attach a gaming console. Besides, consider purchasing a Fire Stick, Roku, or Chromecast if you feel like having more apps on your custom home theater set up. Furthermore, go with a 5 to 9-piece surround sound setup instead of a simple soundbar or stereo speakers. 
Place the Speakers ProperlyThe quality of sound you will have largely decides the quality of the custom home theater experience you will receive. Hence, proper positioning of your speakers is a must. Place the center speaker right under the screen where the dialogues come from. Keep the other two front speakers on the sides of your TV. Moreover, the back speakers should be opposite the front speakers. However, you can keep the subwoofer anywhere in the room and the length of the given wire at your convenience.
Manage the Ambient LightingUnlike other areas of your abode, your home theater room doesn’t need ambient lights or glares from external sources. Instead, you can consider having some shades and curtains utilizable to restrict unwanted lights coming from outside while you enjoy your favorite movies or shows. There you have it; 4 effective tips!

Right from using coffee makers in your kitchen to automated HVAC systems, we are surrounded by custom home theater. So, how about upgrading your smart home game and get an automated garage door? Benefits of Using an Automated Garage Door Just like every other piece of electronic equipment, an automated garage door comes with its own sets of perks. Some of the benefits to reap include: 
  • 1. Convenience Above Anything 
  • One of the primary reasons you should consider using an automatic garage door is its convenience. It not only minimizes your effort in using the door, but you also don’t even have to worry about carrying keys around the house. It can be operated while you are still inside the car. 
  • 2. Better Security 
  • One of the biggest concerns regarding manual garage doors is that it is subjected to robbery. With an automated garage door, you can rest assured, with enhanced security features baked into it. That being said, some of them also come with vacation mode. So, in case you are away from the house, even the remote controller won’t work. 
  • 3. Automatic Garage Doors are Indeed Smart
  • The majority of automatic garage doors out there in the market can be operated using your smartphone. So, you can enjoy the convenience of using your garage door in the comfort of your living room. These garage doors can also be scheduled to close after a certain period. 
  • 4. Child Safety Features 
  • Injuries from falling garage doors account for the majority of mis happenings. But, that is the thing of the past. Sensors attached with automatic garage doors can detect obstacles, be it your child or pet, while closing. So, it can retract back without posing any threat. 
  • 5. Cost-Effective
  • With all the added technology, one might assume garage doors can burn a hole in your pocket. On the contrary, automatic garage doors are not only cost-effective, but they also consume minimal energy while operating.

There is a plethora of options to choose from various products and their dedicated virtual assistant. Here, we will give you an insight into both the advantages and disadvantages of virtual assistants. So, please read below.
Finding the Right Assistant As of now, three of the most acclaimed voice-controlled assistants in the market include Amazon’s Alexa, Google Assistant, and Siri from Apple. While all of them perform similar tasks, still there are several areas where one gets ahead of the other. 
Pros of Amazon Alexa
  • For a seamless smart home integration, Amazon Alexa is just the right fit. 
  • Amazon Echo is always easy to set up and get started.
  • Alexa comes with almost more than 10,000 skill sets, along with compatibility for the third-party voice commands. 
Cons
  • iPhone users might have a hard time setting up their Amazon Echo devices. 
Pros of Google Assistant 
  • It is readily available in all the latest Android smartphones. 
  • It can give both Alexa and Siri a run for their money when it comes to sheer AI. 
  • You can ask a wide range of questions to Google Assistant. 
Cons
  • ‘OK Google’ summoning can get annoying at times. 
Pros of Apple Siri
  • Siri is the most privacy-focused virtual assistant. 
  • It is easier to set up with Apple devices. 
  • Transfer of data between Apple devices takes place in a secured and encrypted environment.
Compatible HardwareNow that you have a clear understanding of the superpowers of each virtual assistant, here is a list of all the hardware they come with. 
  • Alexa: Echo Dot, Standard Echo, and Echo Show
  • Google Assistant: Android Smartphones, Google Home 
  • Siri: iPhone, iPad, and Apple Homepod
The Bottom Line This curated article is on the best virtual assistant you can use for your smart home. Bear in mind, while getting your smart hub, apart from the assistant itself, factors like overall pricing, speakers, and their integration with other appliances play a crucial role. So, once you get everything right, you are always good to go.

LEARN THE RIGHT WAY TO OPTIMIZE YOUR HOME THEATRE EXPERIENCEAre you a movie buff? Are you very meticulous about the experience of watching every movie in the best possible setup and ambiance? Then, having your own dedicated custom home theatre or smart home can be the best solution. With perfect equipment and planning, you can have even a better experience than at the movie theatres. 
The aim is to get a plush movie experience and enjoy the comfort of your private place; at the same time without giving much effort. So, if you are interested in optimizing your smart home theatre experience, here are some more details; to help you plan a perfect setup.
Steps to Design the Ideal Home Theatre for Your Smart HomeThe planning process starts with laying down a realistic budget for your custom home theatre or smart home system. After that, you need to divide the budget into three categories:
  1. Audio-Visual Equipment: The foremost important factor in getting the best audio-visual output is choosing the best equipment; that suits your home design. Buying the largest screen or the most high-end sound systems does not solve all the problems. The screen size and audio output capacity should match your room size; the distance between the screen and audience, furniture and carpeting material, roof height, etc. So, before investing in any equipment, it is better to evaluate these factors by some professionals. 
  1. Room Décor: The room’s decoration in terms of the light arrangement; speakers’ placement, furniture, etc., is the next thing to be taken care of. You need to plan the lighting of a room to avoid unwanted screen glares or surface reflection. The carpeting or the hardwood floor, and the number of furniture along with the wall surface affect the room’s acoustics. 
  1. Seating Arrangements: The seating chairs arrangement, their distance from the screen, the height of a screen, from your eye level makes a big difference; in the entire viewing experience. If you have to strain your eyes or neck for a long time; it will hurt and damage your health over time. 
The Bottom Line Taking care of everything discussed above will not ensure the optimal experience unless you have an intelligent control system for managing all the devices, lights, and everything else while watching the movie. Automation systems like Control4 will give you access to all the smart gadgets in your house within one control device like your smartphones or tablets.
