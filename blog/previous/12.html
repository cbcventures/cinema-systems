WHY SHOULD YOU HIRE EXPERTS TO BUILD A CUSTOM HOME THEATRE SYSTEM IN 2019?
​
Media consumption is rapidly growing, and the same is the quality of movies and series. Streaming services have made it possible to watch it wherever you are. However, are you the type to enjoy the cinematic experience with all the comforts of a theatre?  And how ’bout high-resolution screens, surround sound at your home?  Then, custom home theatre design from the experts is the ideal choice.

THE CINEMATIC EXPERIENCE
Sure, you can watch the same content in a living room couch, but that doesn’t establish the connection to the movie as in theatres. You need to have an immersive experience with nothing but the visual and the audio that can reach you. To take effect, the bright living room is not the right space, you need dedicated big screens, sound systems, recliners and sound sealing to be taken care of which is why opting for a custom home theatre design is recommended.

CHOICE OF EQUIPMENT
The market is flooded with electronics with companies outing newer products every week. This convoluted market space means you are prone to miss out the topmost gear for your home theatre. Hiring experts should make it worry free for you as they can easily pick the best value for your money due to their industry presence.
COMPATIBILITY AND CROSS CONNECTIVITY
Setting up a home theatre is not just buying a screen, sound system, and a couch. It is a lot more than that.  For example, the viewing angle, seating position, and sound reachability.  There are a lot of aspects that common people like as can’t handle. On top of that lies the problems of connecting the electronics equipment to each other. Not to mention, ensuring seamless working and cross connectivity, which is by far the most difficult part.

SOUND SEALING
The most important aspect of a custom home theatre design is the sound sealing that prevents sound leakage in and out of the room. Proper sound sealing is required for an uninterrupted media experience for you. At the same time, don’t disturb your neighbors while you are blasting the speakers with your favorite movie.

AESTHETIC FACTOR
You might have thought home theatres are easy to set with advanced tech equipment. Forget not they need to customize and repaint if required and set in proper location to maintain good looks.
Don’t compromise your movie and gaming experience at your home. It can be achieved within no time with the help of experts, than messing around with cables and woodwork on your own.

IT’S TIME FOR ELDERS TO ADOPT HOME AUTOMATION SYSTEMS

0 Comments

 
IT’S TIME FOR ELDERS TO ADOPT HOME AUTOMATION SYSTEMS
​
Home automation systems can do almost everything from opening your garage door to turning off the lights on your command even when you are not home. Home automation basics have grown strong to the point they can be trusted and installed in every place. Also, most brands are coming up with IoT products for home use. Consequently, you can buy them readily, connect to automation systems, and take control over certain tasks.
WHY SHOULD ELDERS ADOPT HOME AUTOMATION SYSTEMS?
As said before, home automation has become increasingly capable and reliable than a last decade. Home automation basics cover most physical moves required in a home like operating electrical and opening doors. Introduction of home automation can save a lot of physical movement for an elderly person and keep them at rest.
Also, security is a major reason to adopt a home automation system. Along with surveillance cameras, motion detectors are coupled to detect threats and unauthorized entry to alarm the owners and police. In addition, other security reasons like smoke and fire detection are essential.
​
HOW TO ADOPT A HOME AUTOMATION SYSTEM
The home automation system is easy to set. It’s about the light bulbs that change color, and the voice assistant speakers that huge brands sell. They enable automation, but not to the fullest that elders require. To set-up a full-fledged home automation system, you need to call the experts.
  • Know Your Requirements:
    • Find and list down the requirement of home automation by yourself. Creating a checklist to be fulfilled by vendors makes the filtration process easy.
  • Find the Right Home Automation Company:
    • You must be able to spot quite a few home automation contractors in town. Not all build their own home automation platform. Most often they buy external services and assemble them at your place. Look for original makers who can offer better support and long service duration.
  • Installation and Maintenance:
    • Installation should be hassle–free if you have hired an experienced company like Cinema Systems. Maintenance check every 3 months is recommended to keep track of what is working and what is not especially security systems.
  • Security:
    • With automation systems in place, you are safe from external factors. However, the only vulnerability is compromising the security system itself through the Wi-Fi connection. Secure the Wi-Fi router with static IP and a strong firewall or hire someone to set it up for you.
In conclusion, home automation can make life easier and worry free for elders staying alone. If the home automation basics are set right, the future expansions of new features, repair and maintenance are easy. So, get a trusted contractor to work for you.

5 COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID WHILE SETTING UP A CUSTOM HOME THEATER

0 Comments

 
5 COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID WHILE SETTING UP A CUSTOM HOME THEATERThe concept of putting your own custom home theater is quite exciting. The process can be difficult if you are not aware of the full installation process. You can buy the most expensive and branded equipment. However, the wrong installation may end up ruining the set up. People must understand the common home theater mistakes before installing the system.
SELECTING A WRONG ROOMTo get high-quality media performance, one must select the right room. Most of the homeowners select a cramped up very small room or square room. These rooms are not at all conducive for your custom home theater system. The bass waves and the sound waves are irregular in these types of rooms. You can opt for oddly-shaped or rectangular rooms for optimal sound.
NEGATIVE EFFECT ON VISUALSThe position of the home theater and the windows affect the visual performance of your system. The windows having drapes and blinds can also let in some light. This will let the glare effect on your TV. Moreover, the windows do not stop the outside noises from reaching inside your room. This can disrupt your home theater’s performance too.
PURCHASING A WRONG DISPLAY TVWhile setting up the custom home theater system, the display of the TV does matter. If you are purchasing a TV with the biggest display, then it will worsen the view of low-resolution images. The larger TV will not let you watch the entire image from the distance. So, it is better to map out your room and the sitting distance to get an ideal size TV.
PROBLEM WITH THE SOUNDThe small satellite speakers in a large room may end up giving an unimpressive sound. For this, you need to position your speakers in the right location. This will lead to the production of good–quality sound. The homeowners must make sure to buy great quality speakers for their larger rooms. You can choose the floor standing and bookshelf speakers. They will be able to fill in the big empty space.
BUYING IMPROPER ACCESSORIESThis is one of the common mistakes. Mostly, people buy a receiver before proper installation of their home theater system. This will not only cost you money, but it will also be a proper waste. The homeowners must buy the exact accessories only after the installation of their TV.
CONCLUSIONYou must choose the right types of equipment for your custom home theater. This will give you unbeatable media experiences. In case if you have any doubts, then you can consult the experts like Cinema Systems. We help our customer in setting up a home theater and advise them for a better outcome.
