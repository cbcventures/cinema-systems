THE WAR OF SMART HOME VIRTUAL ASSISTANTDo you realize that almost everything is intertwined? This is because custom home theatre or smart homes. It certainly comes with its own sets of downsides as well. For starters, if you want a smart home set-up, finding the right hub can get tedious at times. That’s why you need the right smart home virtual assistant.
There is a plethora of visual assistant to choose from various products and their dedicated virtual assistant. Here, we will give you an insight into both the advantages and disadvantages of virtual assistants. 
Finding the Right Assistant As of now, three of the most acclaimed voice-controlled assistants in the market include Amazon’s Alexa, Google Assistant, and Siri from Apple. While all of them perform similar tasks, still there are several areas where one gets ahead of the other. 
Pros of Amazon Alexa
  • For a seamless smart home integration, Amazon Alexa is the right fit. 
  • Amazon Echo is always easy to set up.
  • Alexa comes with almost more than 10,000 skill sets, along with compatibility for the third-party voice commands. 
ConsiPhone users might have a hard time setting up their Amazon Echo devices. 
Pros of Google Assistant It is readily available in all the latest Android smartphones. 
It can give both Alexa and Siri a run for their money when it comes to sheer AI. 
You can ask a wide range of questions to Google Assistant. 
Cons‘OK Google’ summoning can get annoying at times. 
Pros of Apple SiriSiri is the most privacy-focused virtual assistant. 
It is easier to set up with Apple devices. 
Transfer of data between Apple devices takes place in a secured and encrypted environment.
Compatible HardwareNow that you have a clear understanding of the superpowers of each virtual assistant, here is a list of all the hardware they come with. 
Alexa: Echo Dot, Standard Echo, and Echo Show
Google Assistant: Android Smartphones, Google Home 
Siri: iPhone, iPad, and Apple Homepod
The Bottom Line This curated article is on the best virtual assistant you can use for your smart home. Bear in mind, while getting your smart hub, apart from the assistant itself, factors like overall pricing, speakers, and their integration with other appliances play a crucial role. So, once you get everything right, you are always good to go.

HOW TO SET UP THE ULTIMATE MAN CAVE?It goes without saying, for all the tech-savvy alphas out there, building out a man cave is just as exciting as the next big thing. Like every other home renovation, it requires proper planning to bring forth a perfect visual aesthetic and functionality. So, in this section, we will discuss a curated guide on everything you need to know about setting up your ultimate man cave. 
The Ultimate Man Cave Set Up1. Set Up Plumbing and Electrical Requirements 
The first and foremost task is to plan for all the furniture and accessories required to set up your man cave. Always start with planning out your electrical requirements and the appliances you will be setting up. Next comes installing your sink, especially if you are planning for a full-fledged bar. 
2. Choose a Perfect Theme 
Theme plays a crucial role when it comes to making your man cave visually appealing. Some of the most popular themes include the use of heavy-duty woods and metals. They are not only durable but also look manly as well.

Man caves in the 21st century are all about custom home theater or smart homes. You can always go with a 5.1 or 7.1 surround sound to get the right punch with every beat. Bear in mind, your display of choice should always be in proportion to the overall room size. 
4. Use Proper Lighting
When it comes to setting up the lighting, there are a plethora of options to choose from. But our suggestion would be to go with traditional ceiling lighting instead of LEDs packed with RGB. This, in turn, brings forth the masculinity in the ambiance. 
5. Refrigerators are a Must-Have

6. Don’t Forget Your Air Conditioner

THE BOTTOM LINEThis is our curated guide on everything you need to set up a perfect man cave. So, what are you waiting for? Build a perfect relaxing place you can be proud of.

5.1 VS 7.1 SOUND SYSTEM

9/23/2021

0 Comments

 
CHOOSE A PERFECT HOME THEATER: 5.1 VS 7.1 SOUND SYSTEMWhen it comes to the sensation of hearing, the sound quality preferences gather colossal importance. Thus it’s important to know the difference between 5.1 vs 7.1 systems.. Similarly, the case arises when giving magnificence to custom home theaters where amplifying the interior ambiance is necessary. It is also necessary as to which sound system would preferably fit into the setups of smart homes. In general, if the expansion of the rooms is limited, then the 5.1 would be a perfect match. If the space occupied by the rooms is larger than 30 square meters, the 7.1 system could be employed.
How Do the Features Compare?
  • The 5.1 system mentioned above is configured with 6 loudspeakers, also called the surround speakers, accountable for an atmospheric sound. On the other hand, the 7.1 system has two additional loudspeakers, sometimes known as surround-back speakers. They are implemented to give companion and supplement the atmospheric sound already generated by the surround speakers. 
  • How do you get an efficient sound quality in custom home theater?  The surround speakers have to be placed to the side of the listening position. In contrast, the surround back speakers should be ascended to a certain height. The surround-back speakers are responsible for enhancing the audio ambiance. So they should be placed at a certain distance from the listener. Also a gap of at least 10 cm should be maintained between the speakers and the room confinement. 
  • Furthermore, the processing of both audio and video signals by the A/V receivers will have to support all channels. Being a multi-talented amplifier, it’s feasible to power a 5.1 system with a 7.1 receiver. If someone’s room dimensions are smaller and decide to stay with a 5.1 system setup rather than a 7.1? It would make him cautious about opting for a 5.1 receiver making it an economic investment.
The Bottom LineNowadays, not all movies are mixed for 7.1 sound, and some renowned audio technology companies have developed codes like Dolby Pro Logic IIx and Dolby Atmos, which can mix up 5.1 sound for 7.1 systems in custom home theatre or smart homes. Therefore, choosing a Perfect Home Theatre lies in the space constraints mentioned above. There you have it folks, our comprehensive guide to 5.1 vs 7.1 sound systems.

THE EXPECTED FUTURE OF THE CONCEPT OF SMART HOME WITH SOME OF ITS SURPRISING CAPABILITIESAlthough the concept of smart homes looked a bit lavish at first; it has now become an integral nee of our daily life; because of the gradual adaptation. Backed up by smart plugs, modern gadgets and home appliances connected through home Wi-Fi; these things enable homeowners to control them from remote locations, setting new measures of home usability and ways of convenience. Besides, with the appearance of novel technological inclusions, it’s continuously evolving. So, let’s go through some of the features that the smart home’s future can present us. Let us explore the concept of smart home.
Smart Homes Will Become More Energy EfficientPreserving its aptitude of offering the highest level of convenience to the people living in smart homes, the concept is gradually marching to be more energy efficient. The simultaneous presence of energy generated by electricity, solar, and battery. Automation lets the owners choose the areas of the house..
Also, the automated energy management system saves resources from getting misused. They’re misused by regulating the cooling and heating turning on and off the water, and other home appliances without manual interventions. By tracking the energy consumption of the house, a smart home is learning to make informed decisions. It will also work together with the owner to attain the highest margin of energy efficiency.
Identify Home Owners NeedsThe model of smart homes is being worked on to track the owners. This is done in their house with either electrical sensors implanted in the place or electronic chips on their clothes. The artificial intelligence-powered house will use your real-time location inside the house to serve you on your needs and even foresee them.
Insulation Will Learn to Conserve EnergyWindows are a necessary part of every house because of aesthetics and insulation. However, soon in smart homes, the windows will be seen playing a more critical role, conserving energy and its glasses monitoring the house. The windows are, too, expected to have sensors to judge the weather outside and send the accumulated data to the energy management system, enabling it to regulate the temperature within the home. In addition, it means the house can alter the thermostats on its own.
Smart Locks Will Have Greater EfficiencyThe notion of automation will touch the innovative locking systems of homes more precisely. The owners will be able to program the locks for automatically granting or rejecting visitors without human interferences. Amazon Key would be a real example of such locks.
Wrapping UpThe home integration mechanic is already noticing the appearance of multiple platforms. Hence, Smart Homes are curved again; introducing new facets with superior functional features is only a matter of time.

WHAT DO YOU MEAN BY SMART HOME TECHNOLOGY

0 Comments

 
WHAT DO YOU MEAN BY SMART HOME TECHNOLOGY?When purchasing appliances for our home, we look for the best brand with all the modern features. However, what is the next-generation part? The next generation future in the world of home appliances is intelligent automation technology. If you are wondering what smart technology is, keep reading ahead to explore more!
Smart Home TechnologySmart home technology refers to enabling basic home amenities to work on voice command or remote control. This includes heavy-duty appliances; washing machines, refrigerators, microwave ovens, garage doors, and many more.
Home Entertainment SystemHome security systems involve CCTV surveillance, door and window sensors, face recognition, and voice recognition systems. You can even maintain your home temperature based on external air conditioning, room heating, and lighting.
Smart home technology has opened the doors of the high-tech functional and luxury. With further advancement in technology and the invention of new equipment, consumers’ possibilities make their lives easier. Here are the primary advantages of smart home automation:
  • Smart home offers homeowners to manage all their home appliances and devices from one place. The comfort factor of this feature is enormous. Homeowners need to learn only the functioning of one or two apps before operating the innovative home application, which makes things simpler for them.
  • Smart homes are wonderfully flexible when it comes to new devices and appliances. Regardless of your new devices’ features and state-of-the-art, smart home technologies can easily accommodate new devices by replacing older and outdated ones. The incorporation of these new upgraded models is necessary to experience the best of the class.
  • Enhancing home security. The primary advantage of smart home automation is incorporating the top-notch security and surveillance feature. For example, smart homes can include motion cameras, voice recognition detectors, automated door locks, and light sensors that switch off and close the doors. The advanced systems also help provide the owners with system alerts when someone tries to enter their premises.
  • Energy efficiency is another vital point of innovative home automation technology. Depending on the smart home technology and incorporated devices, homeowners can cut down the hike in bills by replacing human errors with technological advancements. For example, geysers and air conditioners can be controlled using smart home technologies to switch them off after a considerable period. A smart oven can cook foods to perfection without overcooking or undercooking them.
Final ThoughtsWe bet that you will be amazed to see what smart home technologies can do for you. Technological advancement is undoubtedly going to change the future of the living standards of an average person. Intelligent home automation is the first step towards a digital world that will help human beings take control of every action of their lives. We hope you have gained considerable knowledge about the smart home advantages from this article. So, when are you planning to upgrade to a brighter future?

HOW CAN GOOGLE ASSISTANT MAKE YOUR HOME SMART?

0 Comments

 
HOW CAN GOOGLE ASSISTANT MAKE YOUR HOME SMART?Do you have a smart home powered by Google Assistant? Then surely you know it’s programmed enough to perform galore of tasks for its users. But, there are some features most of its users don’t know about. Things are getting interesting. Right?
Regardless of whether you are using AI-powered Google speakers or just your smartphone, all these features are available in both of them. Besides, Google’s extreme search power will be at your side, which has already overlapped Alexa.
So, without spending more time, let’s find some practical ways to utilize Google Assistant.
Locate Your PhoneYou are about to go out, and you cannot find your smartphone. Now, it’s more frustrating than it sounds like.
But, Google Assistant can come to your rescue if a Google Home speaker backs up your smart home. You have to ask your AI Google butler to locate your phone, and it will call your phone up. The same feature is available in mobiles as well.
Voice Commands for Apps OpeningGoing through a busy day and unable to make time for opening your Gmail app to check your next day’s schedule? Just ask your Google Assistant for it.
This feature is beneficial when you are busy planting trees with mud on your hand but have to open the email to check an important email. It also becomes helpful when you have to perform multiple tasks at once.
Freedom From the Noisy Alarm ClocksIf your smart home has a small place for Google Assistant, you don’t have to start your sweet mornings with noisy alarm clocks. And, if you are reluctant to hear regular buzzing alarm tones, your smart assistant can play the song you want from specific playlists.
Alongside morning alarms, the Google Routine will enable you to set several alarms for initiating other actions. Yes! Your benevolent digital assistant can perform several tasks like reading you the newspaper, turn on the lights after the morning alarm, and more.
In a NutshellThat’s the reason it is called an assistant. Don’t you think it’s worthy of this title? Therefore, you should always have it when considering investments for a smart home. Besides, the Google Home Speakers’ assistant can do various other tasks like recognizing your surrounding through the camera, serving you sizzling web shows, send voice messages through voice commands, and more.
Hence, if you don’t have one, gift your home with more smartness by purchasing one as soon as possible. Or, if you have one already, start utilizing these features to derive more convenience from it.
