YOUR FAMILY, POPCORN, & SODA AT YOUR SMART HOME

1/5/2020

0 Comments

 
Get The Best Home Theatre Experience With Your Family, Popcorn, And Soda At Your Smart HomeAt the point of time when the laser playing discs became popular in the market, the individuals began to pair up their crisp, new picture with stereo sound to make a memorable experience dissimilar to anything else. As time passed by, home theater technology has advanced, and converged with smart home. Individuals are now able to appreciate a full film sitting back at their home.
A few essential things are to be considered while designing a perfect home theater. Moreover, it is important to design each progression to ensure you cut your cloth according to your pocket.
Pool your assets and the effort to assemble a home theatre and build a smart home for you and your family to enjoy for a long time.
How to Choose your Theatre Room to Get the Best Effect?The location of your home theatre is the most crucial thing to consider first. A home theatre room is different from a media room, as it serves additional purposes other than just watching TV shows and movies. For the best results, choose a square or rectangular room with a low ceiling and no exterior windows. Hard floors generate unwanted echoes and uneven bass, so it is recommended to have a carpeted floor in your smart home, which helps in absorbing the unwanted sound echoes.
How to Determine the Size of a Video Display?The size of a room will decide the size and type of video gadget (TV or projector) that would be ideal. The seating place you introduce should be comfortable and urge individuals to stay in one spot. Your home theatre seating distance ought to be 1.5 times the size of a screen. If the seating distance from the screen is 9 feet, then install at most a 6 feet (diagonally) video display.
How to Get the Right Equipment for your Home Theatre?To get an ideal home theater equipment, stay with the best brands in the market. A large number of top producers offer packages, incorporating projection and sound systems that are optimized to work well together.
How to Create a Perfect Sound Effect at your Theatre?The next most essential element is the Dolby Digital sound. For this, consult any sound engineer to get the best solution. Generally, it is recommended to get a 3D sound effect by placing five to seven speakers with sub-woofers in front, above, and backside of the  
viewers.
How to Control All the Gadgets Together?A complete home theatre must include a built-in full controlling system to adjust the volume, theatre room lights, and thermostat. The top brands provide the required in-built control devices to have a thrilling experience.
This complete guide on purchasing an excellent home theater will make your sweet home a smart home.

UNDERSTANDING IN-CEILING AND IN-WALL SPEAKERS IN HOME THEATERSSetting up your dream home theater sometimes indulges in a better and more innovative way. You may have a feel of setting up an in-wall or in-ceiling speakers in your place. And the reason may be anything. Notwithstanding, this may be because you might feel the room is little cramped for space, or maybe because of the style factor. Whatever it is,  speakers have come up to age and provide a better sound experience to the users overall. After all, innovation makes life a lot better, adding to the overall benefits.
In-wall and In-Ceiling speakers have their advantages. Before making a purchase, understand some valuable tips that are committed to serving the best for your home theaters.
  • With advanced technology at helm, 5.1 music systems are concealable inside a wall ceiling. With technology’s promise, the day is not far, where 7.1 systems appear in these forms.
  • In case of both these speakers, the design frame is entirely responsible for the sound output given by the system. In-ceiling speakers use the wall or the ceiling as a cabinet, which sends back the sound bass surround in the room.
  • In the case of in-wall speakers, the black box is responsible for acting as the bass generator. However, there is a small issue with this particular type of speaker, is you will not know how they will sound.
  • Both in-ceiling and in-wall speakers come with adjustable treble tone controls and dedicated bass, helping your speakers work with the rest of speakers.
  • Both speakers require at least 4–inch clearance, right next to the mounting panel, and this assures the spreading of sound waves clearer.
  • In-wall speakers come up in a rectangular frame, whereas in-ceiling speakers are round. However, these are just shapes and do not hamper sound quality by any means. Moreover, these are paintable and do not be damaged with different emulsions.
ConcludingIn-ceiling and In-wall speakers have greatly managed to change how people looked at having a beautiful home theater systems. These speakers add to the beautification of the room and do not hamper the quality of sound by any means. Unlike regular speakers, these have different types of mounts, which adds to the bass effect. If you are unsure which one to buy, call the experts of Cinema System. OLED vs LED: Which TV To Choose For Your Home Theater?So OLED vs LED, which one is the winner?  Owning a home theater is a dream for many people today. After all, these are some of the best ways to get entertained and experience a feel of watching stuff in your private theater. While you may be aware of completing the setup, certain avenues might keep you guessing. One of the main areas of concern surrounds on which TV to choose among LED and OLED.


Since both of these TV types have proven their worth repeatedly, it is a possibility you might get confused on the one to choose. With both having their plus and minuses, here are some of the more natural ways, guaranteeing you to get a clear picture, including our perspective of choices. Without further ado, here are the vantage points of both of these TV types done considering essential aspects,


BrightnessLED: This is popular in this segment and offers extreme bright light to the viewers. Even more so, the addition of quantum dots allows the brightness to be more eye catchy.


OLED: Organic LED TV does not boast extreme brightness in a normal condition. In comparison, however, on a dark room, OLED may slightly outscore LED, but on a brighter condition, LED is a clear winner.


Winner: LED


Response Time SegmentLED: These TV’s use LCD panel and illuminate the clusters instead. Therefore, the diodes in LED TVs operate slower than OLED.


OLED: OLED comprises smaller diodes, working on single pixels. OLED offers the fastest and the swiftest operation among the TV of this generation.


Winner: OLED


Viewing AnglesLED: Unsurprisingly though, LED falls short in this segment, giving a maximum viewing angle of 54 degrees at most. This suggests LED has a dead center as the best viewing directions. On moving sideways, the picture quality and contrasts fall drastically with these TV.

UNDERSTANDING DTS AND DOLBY SOUND: COMPARATIVE STUDY

10/24/2019

0 Comments

 
UNDERSTANDING DTS AND DOLBY SOUND: COMPARATIVE STUDYFor a significant number of home theaters today, there are specific prerequisites.  Demanding certain things for gaining the best experience is one example. Among them, the sound is undoubtedly one of the most important things to consider. While considering it in the first place, DTS and Dolby Sound comes to mind. Both sounds are known for providing the much–needed surround sound for the theater experience.
Before proceeding to the comparison part, there have been speculations by few users on DTS.  Some giving a considerably better surround sound, thanks to its higher bit rate over Dolby. Regardless of these points, more points to consider while comparing these systems individually.
What are DTS and Dolby Sounds?Surround sounds are one of responsible for making a home theater lively. Most of the top home theater has either of one sound system.  They are typically either Dolby Digital 5.1 and of DTS 5.1 variants. Have you wondered what is so different about these systems?
Dolby Digital technology is one of the first systems to incorporate surround sound systems. The system has reached heights with different innovation and sound amplification technologies. The USP of Dolby sounds the systems comprising low–frequency channel against higher bandwidth.
DTS technology came a little later and pushed the technology of using higher bit rate, while distributing sound waves. DTS is known to have more audio information, suitable for theater like experience.
With both these sound formats having a go at each other, here are some similarities to look up,
  • Both technologies are suited for advanced custom home theater system. With codecs available for 5.1, 6.1, and 7.1 home theaters, users will have a gala of a time.
  • Both technologies are known to compress dense audio waves and all of the appliances. TV, music players, and Video systems support them. The receiver during playback, however, decompresses the same, allowing better and loud sound experience.
DifferencesTalking about the sound channel and bit rate, Dolby Digital only supplies 5.1 channel at 640 kbit/s. Whereas, DTS gives the same at a whopping 1.5 Mbit/s.
Dolby TrueHD, a system for High Definition music, gives out a whopping 18 Mbit/s lossless quality. On the contrary, DTS HD Master Audio, the equivalent from DTS gives out lossless quality at 24.5 Mbit/s.

HOW TO SMART YOUR HOME WITHOUT SPENDING MUCH

0 Comments

 
HOW TO SMART YOUR HOME WITHOUT SPENDING MUCH?

Home is all about convenience. Everyone would like more comfort in the form of a smart home. However, how do you plan to smart your home? Ripping down the whole home system and starting again is not a good idea, and considering the cost that is not wise either. You will already have security cameras and smoke detectors in place, but how do we make it work together and make it accessible wherever you are. We have answers for all your questions through simple techniques and workarounds that do not cost much.
Start with assessing your existing home systemsSecurity system, fire and smoke alarm system, HVAC, and music system may be part of your smart ecosystem. Assessing means getting to know their compatibility to communicate with other devices like a modem or a smart home hub, ports available, functionalities, etc. This forms the basis of your minimal, intelligent home setup plan. The objective is to use as much of existing gadgets to keep the costs low.
List the features you expect for your smart home.For example, some of the essential elements of an intelligent home setup are,
  • Virtual voice assistant
  • Temperature control
  • Light on/off and color change
  • Viewing camera from anywhere
  • Notifications on abnormal activities from security cameras
Making a list of your existing systems and the required functionality will narrow down your search from there on.
DecisionThe smart home ecosystem is the most significant decision you will need to make while you are in the planning stage. The vital ecosystems are the Google Home, Amazon Alexa, and Samsung. Wanting to add smart features to existing devices, the compatibility of existing devices and the availability of newer gadgets under your budget should be your deciding factors.
Fix a smart hub and connect as many existing gadgets to the hub to make it functional and accessible the smart way. While there are not many devices from the past compatible with the intelligent hub, security cameras can be connected for live video streaming across the internet that can be accessed even when you are not home. Another minimal costing upgrade is the addition of smart plugs to turn on/off appliances.
When the improvising part of existing tools and gadgets are done, now you can add on other necessary smart home devices. Smart cloud cam is an inexpensive security tool that adds value to your smart home setup. Likely, there are smart thermostats, smart multi–color LED, and smart locks that do not cost a fortune. From now on the addition of smart home gadgets is endless. You can keep adding devices whenever you wish to.
