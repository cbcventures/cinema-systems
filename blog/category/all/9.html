CUSTOMIZE THE AUDIO ON TV TO HEAR

12/23/2020

0 Comments

 
HOW TO CUSTOMIZE THE AUDIO IN TV TO HEAR DIALOGUES WITH CLARITYHome theaters are great and can definitely complement your movie-watching experience. However, when you want to listen to the specific dialogues, you may have to raise the volume. It may not work because the sound might go up without specifically raising the crispness of the audio.
How to customize this audio in the television to get crystal clear audio, so you hear even the gentlest mutterings perfectly well? Instead of breaking your head, follow these little tips to ensure you get the problem solved.
Check the TV’s Sound SettingsIn your flat screen television, look for the Dynamic Range Compression or Control and keep it in the maximum. When you set it to the highest, the audio’s minutest details get higher. It will, however, allow the television not to give automatically off loud explosive sounds when there is an action scene. In some televisions, it can be under Night Mode options too.
Equalization AdjustmentEQ levels have to be proper, and the bass and treble controls should be aligned. If the dialogues sound muffled, reduce the bass level and the treble for a slight but noticeable improvement. This equalizer will have a set of frequencies for you to amplify or reduce. Get more bass by heightening the lower frequencies. There are two-stage, bass, and treble frequency control. There are more options to manage the frequencies. Remember that you cannot do much when your home system does not have all the speakers functioning.
Replace Speakers with Better UpgradesThis poor speaker quality brings us to this point. Here you have to go for a set of new speakers with better sound. Try to connect it to the TV and check the amps. These will have analog stereo RCA connections. If your speakers also have coaxial or optical digital audio inputs, then also it can work.
Make the Soundbar System ConnectionThese are compact units with built-in amplifiers and speakers. Make sure to connect the soundbar and television. These will receive the audio from the TV via HDMI ARC and optical connections. These soundbars are also great if you want to play games on your TV.
Central Channel OutputThe surround system s going to help you since most of the movies have a multi-channel configuration. The central speaker is the one you need to focus on to get the dialogues clear and the low-frequency sounds clear.
The overall adjustment requires a little bit of checking with the in-built systems, equalizers, available sound modes, and more. These and a little bit of speaker-by-speaker adjustment should be able to give you the best clarity.

Home Theater Seating Ideas: What You Need To Know?Watching a movie on a home theater is an experience in itself. With friends and family together, you can very well enjoy your weekend. Along with the custom home theater, you need a proper seating arrangement not to ruin your movie day. Now, for your seating arrangement, do not end up spending a fortune that seems like clutter. Work your way intelligently according to the space available around your home theater.
You need to see your budget and plan the seating arrangement. Moreover, accessories and material also play a vital role. It is necessary to make one comfortable.
Let us now see a few tips to make a great home theater environment.
Home Cinema Seating ArrangementFor creating this type of seating arrangement, you can buy the regular and high-quality leather recliner and modify it accordingly. Here, you can add cup holders, headrests, and footrests. Another benefit of getting a leather recliner is it looks stylish and even adds a sense of luxury. Home Theater with a ThemeSome of you want to recreate your home theater with a theme that looks quite different from a simple home theater look. You can not only get the custom home theater, but your room can be changed into your favorite movie theme, environmental, cinematic period, etc. themes. Well, you can go with a superhero theme too. It will not dig a hole in your pocket, plus you can entertain your family, friends, or enjoy a great date night too.

HOW TO GET A CUSTOM HOME THEATER ON A BUDGET?Buying a home theater and getting it installed can be an expensive process for homeowners. A few of us may not be aware of the budget to set up a custom home theater, but with the right balance and choosing the right things, one can set up the home theater at a reasonable cost too. You can even repurpose things and avoid mistakes to purchase low-quality pieces of equipment. GUIDE FOR BEGINNERS FOR INSTALLING HOME THEATER AUDIO

0 Comments

 
A QUICK GUIDE FOR BEGINNERS FOR INSTALLING HOME THEATER AUDIO SYSTEMWatching movies right from the comfort of your home is fascinating, and so is to install a custom home theater. But, for enjoying a great film and thrilling sounds, you need to possess a great audio system. For beginners, it may be a little tedious to choose the right speakers to fill the room with audible sounds. So, it’s necessary to analyze your room’s space and then purchase the audio system for installation. Here is a sneak peek; a few tips that will help beginners to install a proper and budgeted audio system for your home theater.
Set Up AmplifierThe hi-fi surround system for home theater in your room is available through the stereo sound or an amplifier. Get the speakers that fit into the area of the room with an all-in-one home theater system and an amplifier. However, you will also require a DVD or a Blu-ray player to attach your surround system. You must invest in the surround speakers as this will help in providing an exceptional audio experience.
Get Multi-Channel Speaker SystemNow, this is what is required to get a full auditory surround system in your home theater set up the room. It includes a center speaker, two front speakers, two surround speakers, and the sub-woofer. The sub-woofer can be placed anywhere in the room as it does not emit the high directional signals, but speakers must be placed in the right place.
Connect With an AV ReceiverAn AV Receiver also works as a multi-channel amplifier. It can connect and power up to two stereo speakers. Moreover, it can connect a minimum of six speaker connections. This multi-channel amplifier will take several audio inputs from the input devices. You can use the analog connections or HDMI connections to connect the AV Receiver. Besides, it will produce sounds from the central or side speakers as per your choice. You can get the video cable connected to your custom home theater that will help you watch the images along with listening to the audio.
So, these are a few accessories that you will require to install a custom home theater system in your chosen space. By choosing the right accessories and equipment, you can save a great deal on the full home theater system. For getting a tremendous auditory sound, connect the speaker with the TV properly, and get an AV Receiver that will help you not to get extra cables or devices.

FLAT SCREEN DISPLAY OR PROJECTORS?

10/31/2020

0 Comments

 
FLAT SCREEN DISPLAY OR PROJECTORS? WHICH IS IDEAL FOR YOUR HOMEIf you want to upgrade your home theater viewing, what are the options you plan to check? Do you want to pick a big flat-screen display monitor? Or do you want to check the projectors for the same? Projectors are coming at lesser prices these days. At the same time, even the HD Flat screen monitors are at less price. 
Let us help you assimilate all the points and pick the best display screen for the best home-viewing. 
Size of the screen:These days, it is common to come across 85-inch television screens. It is close to the projector screen size. So, if you were planning to go for the projector just for this wide-screen perspective, then you have an option. 
Cost:The cost of wide-screen television has come low. This is thanks to the plethora of brands selling the same in the market. Smart TVs are in a rage, and there are televisions available at around $2000 to $4000 approximately. On the other hand, the projector screens can come just within $1000, making it a clear-cut choice for budget shoppers.
Brightness Factor:Whether you watch the television in a dark room or in a brightly-lit room, brightness will stand out. It will not be a problem for you. However, to use the projector indoors, you will need to watch it in a dark room to get the perfect contrast. The LED TVs measure their brightness in nits, and the projectors with their bulbs are in no way anywhere closer in a match with the LED screens. 
Blue Light:You must be aware of the blue light-emitting out of all the devices we use; from smartphones to laptops to even flat-screen TVs. However, this blue light can damage the eyes on long-term exposure leading to even retina’s damage. On the other hand, the projectors also emit these blue light. This does not harm the eyes as directly as the TVs. 
Resolution:Agreed these days, you get 4K HDR projectors. But if you want to know their resolution is as good as the 4K TVs, the answer is no! The actual experience of watching a movie with the Ultra HD Blu-ray player on the HD television is more worth it. 
There are always ways to optimize the home theater by upgrading and customizing well within your budget. We hope you shall now be able to compare the display monitor before opting for either of them for your home theater.

HOW TO CHOOSE A SUBWOOFER FOR HOME THEATER

10/20/2020

0 Comments

 
HOW TO CHOOSE A SUBWOOFER FOR HOME THEATERDo you want to amplify the home theater experience by a zillion times more and in a budget? Yes, the best way to achieve the same is by going for smaller upgrades. For instance, invest in a set of high-quality subwoofers. It will bring in a strong bass-impact and can give you excellent audio output. 
The big challenge for many is much more than just this recommendation. The question that now arises is how to find the best subwoofer for your home theater. Here are a few tips to help you select the same. 
Power:If you look for a subwoofer with amplification built-in, you will notice that you need not get an extra amp for the same. However, look for the subwoofer with higher amplification. It will increase your chances of getting a more resonant bass and a better subwoofer’s performance overall. 
Low-Frequency Extension:The subwoofers can produce the best bass. But some subwoofers will not be able to make it lower than the 20Hz. To ensure the listeners do not notice this, they raise the mid-bass levels. In short, you hear a booming noise. The best aspect, in this case, would be to invest in superior brands of subwoofers only. 
Great Frequency Response:You must be able to hear the accurate pitch, and there should not be any extra distortion happening. You may not even have to raise the volume to the maximum to achieve this. It would be best if you looked for clear flattened notes but come out with absolute sharpness and clarity. 
Choose as per Room Size:A big mistake many people do in this regard is to go for the subwoofer that does not suit the room size. Look for the products that leave the real world footprints. It should not affect regular traffic flow in the room and not be taking up space in the room just like that. Cylindrical models are also space-saving. 
Get Passive Radiators:When you are already getting a sub-woofer, try to get the passive radiators as well. These work in sync with the driver and can add the much-needed style in audio clarity without affecting the output or without increasing the sub amplifier’s size. 
There are quite a few such pointers that you ought to check and be doubly sure while getting subwoofers. Make sure to have an audiophile’s help if you need better products for your home theater.

5 MISTAKES TO AVOID ON CUSTOM HOME THEATER

9/10/2020

0 Comments

 
5 MISTAKES TO AVOID WHEN SETTING UP A CUSTOM HOME THEATERWant to know 5 mistakes to avoid on custom home theater setup? A personal home theater is an excellent addition to your dream home. However, it adds value, and you can enjoy a fun time with their friends and family only when it is set up correctly. Another great thing about possessing a custom home theater is you can quickly watch a movie of your liking, no need to visit the cinema hall, and yes, you can avoid that peak-hour traffic and rush too. You do not have to call a professional even to set up the home theater if you avoid these common pitfalls. Read below to know how to avoid a few common yet big mistakes.
Opt for the Wrong RoomChoose the right room to avoid any media performance. Cramped up or square rooms are not the right fit for the custom home theater. To get the right media and sound, you can go ahead with the rectangular or oddly shaped rooms.
Purchase the Wrong SpeakersSpeakers are a necessary part of the home theater, but people spend a higher amount on branded home theater speakers. One must spend money on suitable speakers that go well with your home theater. So, choose the speakers that fit your room area, and there is a space for sub-woofers as well.
Get a Wrong DisplayMap your whole room space before getting the TV display for it. Just getting a large TV with low-resolution is going to solve the purpose. Moreover, a larger TV will not let you watch the images properly from a distance too. A 4K Ultra HD TV can solve your home theater viewing experience. 
Door’s LocationKeep this critical aspect in mind while setting up the custom home theater. Well, who would like people passing in front of the screen to get the eatables from the kitchen or going to the next room to attend the call? So, place your home theater in the room where there is a TV space or create a seating arrangement in such a way that people can go to other rooms to get the things they require.
Lighting in the RoomYou may be witnessing blurred or low-quality images because of the glare falling on your screen. Get the blackout shades, black or gray screens to eliminate this problem. You can even get the projector that has the “higher lumen count” to see clear images on the screen.
Getting a custom home theater and setting it up can be a great experience. By avoiding these little mistakes, you can enjoy a great ambiance like that of a movie hall just in the comfort of your home.

5 TIPS TO SAFEGUARD YOUR SMART HOME FROM HACKERS

7/9/2020

0 Comments

 
5 Tips To Safeguard Your Smart Home From HackersIt is now a priority to safeguard your smart home. The demand for smart homes is increasing day by day among the users. According to Statista, the smart home market will witness revenue of nearly US$27,649 million by the year 2020. It makes smart homes a first choice for the homeowners nowadays, as it makes their life easier, by operating their appliances, lights, doors, etc. with a click on their Smartphone.
But, with technological advantages come many difficulties too like hacking. The Smart devices present in your home can become vulnerable to attack from hackers. Here are a few tips to safeguard your smart home from the hackers.
Use Unique PasswordsThe key to protecting your high-tech devices is a strong password. It is necessary to create a unique password for all the smart devices to avert the problem of hacking. It is cumbersome to remember all the unique passwords, so save them in the password manager. This tool can also help you to create distinct passwords as well as manage them across all the smart devices in your home.
Protect Wi-FiAnother way through which hackers can hack your home is through your Wi-Fi. Protect the gadgets from cyber criminals with strong passwords, including letters, numbers, and symbols. A strong Wi-Fi password must be created using upper and lower case characters instead of birthdates, children’s names, etc.
Update your Smart DevicesThe software updates in your smart devices will help you to keep your device safe from any vulnerability. Moreover, adjust your security settings and schedule the updates too. Installing an updated malware and anti-virus software also helps in securing your smart home.
Check Router SecurityHome Routers are a critical IoT device that is vulnerable to hacking. The essential tip is to keep your router safe from any intrusion. Always use encryptions or complicated types of passwords to make your router safe. The main reason being it will safeguard the information traveling through your high-speed internet data provider through your router. Two-step authentication will also save it from hackers.
Go for Professional InstallationIf you are not habitual of dealing with smart devices integration in your smart home, opt for
professional installation services. The professionals are well versed with modern technologies and handle all your smart devices related queries quickly.
These are a few essential tips to help you safeguard your smart home from hacking. If you follow them responsibly, it will make your life happier and more comfortable. But, a thoughtful step can help you to protect your home from hackers.
